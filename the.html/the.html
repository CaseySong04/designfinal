Hong Seonghwa

Original Works

Hong Seonghwa captures the intricacies of the human spirit in her paintings, while re-contextualising natural symbols like lotuses and peonies for a contemporary world. Human emotion is central to her works, and moments of joy, sorrow, or hardship are all explored as reflections of the self.
